Turmeric

Turmeric serves against chronic pain and inflammation. Treats inflammatory conditions, skin diseases, wounds, digestive ailments, and liver conditions. Tumeric eases joint pain, and supports your immune system.

Saffron

Saffron is used for asthma, cough, and to loosen phlegm. It is used to improve sleep, treat cancer, depression, heartburrn. Saffron boost heart health, increase circulation, prevent diabetes, strengthen bones, and improve the immune function.

Saffron

Boswellia

Boswellia, also known as Indian frankincense, is an herbal extract taken from the Boswellia serrata tree. Boswellia is used to treat osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, asthma, inflammatory bowel disease.

Boswellia

Ginger

Ginger is a flowering plant that originated from China. Ginger has anti-inflammatory, anti-diabetic, antioxidant properties. It is effective against nausea. It reduces muscle pain. It is helpful against chronic indigestion.

Ginger

Amalaki

Amalaki supports antioxidant activity and healthy skin. Amalaki is well-known as a natural source of vitamin C. It is shown to support heart and arterial health, healthy blood sugar levels, and cholesterol levels.

Amalaki

Arjuna

Arjuna has been used in India for more than 3000 years. It is known for supporting cardiovascular health.It is also used as “a water pill,” and for earaches, dysentery, sexually transmitted diseases (STDs), diseases of the urinary tract, and to increase sexual desire.

Arjuna

Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha is used for arthritis, anxiety, balance, trouble sleeping (insomnia), tumors, tuberculosis, asthma, leukoderma, bronchitis, fibromyalgia, menstrual problems, It is also used to reduce side effects of medications used to treat cancer and schizophrenia.

Ashwagandha

Gymnema

Gymnema is a woody climbing shrub native to India and Africa. The leaves are used to make medicine. It is considered to have anti-diabetic properties improving blood sugar. It improves cholesterol, aids weight loss, helps reduce inflammation.

Gymnema

Triphala

Triphala contains vitamin C, helps against heart disease, has antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, helps with fat loss.

Triphala

Guggul

Guggul helps with weight loss. It serves as a good remedy for skin disorders such as acne. Guggul is the common name for the flowering mukul myrrh tree (Commiphora mukul). It is a natural source of antioxidants, supports the immune system, promotes detoxification and rejuvenation.

Guggul

Licorice

Licorice is taken by mouth for various digestive system complaints including stomach ulcers, heartburn, colic, and ongoing inflammation of the lining of the stomach (chronic gastritis). Some people take licorice by mouth for sore throat, bronchitis, cough, and infections caused by bacteria or viruses. Information taken from Licoirice

Licorice

Shatavari

Shatavari is a species of asparagus plant. It is believed to improve resistance to stress, reduce symptoms of menopause, protect the body from harm caused by free radicals, combat anxiety and depression.

Shatavari

Neem

Neem is an effective anti-viral and anti-bacterial herb. Neem stimulates immune system, and promotes healing to where viral infection could take place. Neem promotes healthy skin and a good complexion, healthy respiratory system, and metabolism.. The herb is a great immune booster.

Neem

Haritaki

Haritaki is a rich source of potent antioxidants like ellagic acid, gallic acid, selenium and vitamin C that protect your body from cancer and other serious diseases. Haritaki fruit also has strong antibacterial and antiviral properties for preventing infection from bacteria in the food you eat and from airborne viruses.Haritaki

Haritaki

Trikatu

Trikatu is an Ayurvedic blend of equal parts of the fruits of Black Pepper (Piper nigrum), Long Pepper (Piper longum) and the rhizomes of Ginger (Zingiber officinale). Trikatu is stimulative in nature, good for liver, spleen and pancreas. It helps in healing various respiratory and digestive diseases.

Trikatu

Ajwain

Ajwain seeds are rich in fiber, minerals, vitamins and antioxidants. Ajwain is used for stomachache, cold, asthma, kidney stone. It has anesthetic, antibacterial, and antifungal properties.

Ajwain

Bibhitaki

Bibhitaki is useful for hair growth and eye nourishment. It hasmany essential vitamins and minerals. Particularly it is riched in Vitamin C, manganese, potassium, iron and copper. Bithitaki is rejuvenating in nature.

Bibhitaki

Aloe Vera contains 20 amino acids which are required by humans, out of which 7-8 are essential amino acids. It is know for it's anti-inflammatory properties. It consists of various enzymes which assist in catabolism of fats and carbs.

Cinnamon

Cinnamon relieves cough with sputum, good for heart, bladder, and lungs, relieves headaches, cleanses throut, soothes and upset stomache, clears urinary tract infactions, kills many disease causing fungi and viruses.

Cardamom

Cardamom removes excess Kapha from stomach and lungs. It is used for common cold and other infections, cough, bronchitis, sore mouth and throat, urinary problems, epilepsy, headache, and high blood pressure.
